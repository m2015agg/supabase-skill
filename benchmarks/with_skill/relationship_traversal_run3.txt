Two tables have foreign keys pointing to `plans`:

| Table | Column | Via FK |
|-------|--------|--------|
| `plan_days` | `plan_id` | → `plans.id` |
| `plan_blocks` | `plan_day_id` | → `plans.id` (indirect, through plan_days) |

Note: `plan_blocks` technically points to `plan_days.plan_day_id`, not directly to `plans.id` — the supabase-skill inferred the relationship. Direct FK references to `plans.id` are only from `plan_days`.

There are also name-related tables with no declared FK constraint: `plan_block_types`, `plan_day_feedback`, `plan_prompt_recipes`, `plan_rag_recipes`.
