Two tables have foreign keys pointing to `plans`:

| Table | FK Column | Notes |
|-------|-----------|-------|
| `plan_days` | `plan_days.plan_id → plans.id` | The daily schedule entries for a plan |
| `plan_blocks` | `plan_blocks.plan_day_id → plans.id` | Individual content blocks (note: points via `plan_day_id`, likely chained through `plan_days`) |

The `plans` table itself has one outbound FK: `block_recipe_id → plan_block_recipes.id`.
