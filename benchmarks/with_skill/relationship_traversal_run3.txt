Two tables have foreign keys pointing to `plans`:

| Table | Column | FK Reference |
|-------|--------|--------------|
| `plan_days` | `plan_id` | → `plans.id` |
| `plan_blocks` | `plan_day_id` | → `plans.id` (indirect, via plan_days) |

Note: `plan_blocks` actually references `plan_days.plan_day_id`, not `plans.id` directly — the schema output shows it pointing to `plans.id` which may be a display quirk. The direct FK chain is `plans → plan_days → plan_blocks`.

`plans` itself has one outbound FK: `block_recipe_id → plan_block_recipes.id`.
