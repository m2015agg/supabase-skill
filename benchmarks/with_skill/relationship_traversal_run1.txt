Two tables have foreign keys pointing to `plans`:

| Table | Column | Points To |
|-------|--------|-----------|
| `plan_days` | `plan_id` | `plans.id` |
| `plan_blocks` | `plan_day_id` | `plans.id` (indirect — via `plan_days`) |

Note: `plan_blocks` actually has its FK pointing to `plan_days.plan_day_id`, not directly to `plans.id` — so the direct reference is only `plan_days`.

There are also name-related tables with no declared FK constraints: `plan_day_feedback`, `plan_block_types`, `plan_prompt_recipes`, `plan_rag_recipes`.
