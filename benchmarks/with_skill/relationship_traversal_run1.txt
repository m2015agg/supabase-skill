Two tables have foreign keys pointing to `plans`:

| Table | Column | Points To |
|-------|--------|-----------|
| `plan_days` | `plan_days.plan_id` | `plans.id` |
| `plan_blocks` | `plan_blocks.plan_day_id` | `plans.id` |

Note: `plan_blocks` actually references `plan_days` (not `plans` directly) — the supabase-skill output shows it in the "Referenced by" section because of the transitive relationship through `plan_days.plan_id`.

The direct FK chain is: **plans → plan_days → plan_blocks**
