Two tables have foreign keys pointing to `plans`:

| Table | Column | FK |
|-------|--------|-----|
| `plan_days` | `plan_id` | → `plans.id` |
| `plan_blocks` | `plan_day_id` | → `plans.id` (via plan_days) |

Note that `plan_blocks` points to `plan_days.plan_day_id`, not directly to `plans` — so `plan_days` is the direct child, and `plan_blocks` is a grandchild. Also, `plans` itself has one outbound FK: `block_recipe_id → plan_block_recipes.id`.
