Two tables have foreign keys pointing to `plans`:

| Table | FK Column | Points To |
|-------|-----------|-----------|
| `plan_days` | `plan_id` | `plans.id` |
| `plan_blocks` | `plan_day_id` | `plans.id` (indirect — via plan_days) |

Note: `plan_blocks.plan_day_id` technically points to `plan_days`, not directly to `plans` — so only **`plan_days`** has a direct FK to `plans.id`.

Also worth knowing: `plan_day_feedback`, `plan_prompt_recipes`, and `plan_rag_recipes` are name-related but have no declared FK constraints.
