Two tables have foreign keys pointing to `plans`:

| Table | Column | References |
|-------|--------|------------|
| `plan_days` | `plan_id` | `plans.id` |
| `plan_blocks` | `plan_day_id` | `plans.id` (via plan_days) |

`plan_days` directly references `plans.id` via `plan_id`. `plan_blocks` references `plan_days.plan_day_id`, so it's an indirect reference one level down.

Also noted: `plans` itself has one outgoing FK — `block_recipe_id → plan_block_recipes.id`.
